    Origin Story

    Joe Bob had always loved Thai food. He loved the complex flavors, the use of fresh herbs and spices, and the balance between sweet, sour, salty, and spicy. As he grew older, he began to dream of opening his own Thai restaurant, where he could share his love of this cuisine with others.


    Today, H-Thai-ML is still going strong, located at 1233 Walt Lane in Las Vegas, NV 89118. Joe Bob has expanded the menu, added new dishes, and even opened four locations. But no matter how big H-Thai-ML gets, it will always be the result of Joe Bob's passion for Thai food and his determination to share that passion with the world.
